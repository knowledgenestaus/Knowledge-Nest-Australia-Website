Our Values

Grounded in cognitive science, implementation science, and the latest educational research to ensure every recommendation is backed by evidence.

Human-Centred

Recognising that lasting change happens when people feel supported, respected, and empowered throughout the improvement journey.

Future-Focused

Building capacity that endures beyond the engagement - equipping educators with the knowledge and skills to lead continuous improvement.

Mission Statement

Knowledge Nest exists to bridge the gap between research and practice.

Grounded in evidence, we equip schools and families with practical tools and support to grow confident, capable learners.

We nurture early success to spark motivation, awaken curiosity, and inspire ambition - empowering children to see their potential.

When knowledge nests are carefully built, aspirations hatch...
and futures take flight.

"Bridging the divide between research and practice while maintaining the human heart of education."

What we do

Tailored support across six key areas of educational improvement

Science of Learning

Applying cognitive science principles to strengthen instructional practice and improve student outcomes across all learning areas.

Science of Reading

Supporting schools to implement structured literacy approaches that align with the evidence on how children learn to read.

Knowledge-Rich Curriculum

Helping schools design and implement curricula that build deep, connected knowledge across year levels and disciplines.

MTSS

Guiding schools to build multi-tiered systems of support that ensure every student receives the right level of instruction and intervention.

Change Management & Implementation Science

Guiding schools through the complexities of instructional change with a structured, evidence-informed approach that respects the human experience of reform.

Explicit Instruction

Building adaptive and responsive teaching practices aligned with the science of learning, incorporating multi-tiered systems of support for every learner.
