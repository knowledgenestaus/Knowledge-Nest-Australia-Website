Services

How we work

Tailored support designed to meet your school or system wherever you are on your improvement journey.

Service formats

Flexible engagement options to suit your context and goals

1:1 Coaching

Personalised coaching for teachers and leaders - building capacity through regular, reflective conversations grounded in evidence and practice.

Professional Learning

Interactive workshops and seminars that translate research into practical, actionable strategies for teachers and leadership teams.

Long-Term Partnerships

Sustained, embedded support over a term or year - working alongside your team to drive deep, lasting instructional improvement.

Professional learning formats

Sessions designed to fit your schedule and professional learning needs

Short Meeting Sessions

Focused 45-60 minute sessions ideal for staff meetings or team planning time. These bite-sized sessions introduce a key concept or strategy that teachers can apply immediately in their practice.

Half-Day PL

A 2-3 hour deep dive into a specific topic or area of practice. Half-day sessions allow time for exploring the research, collaborative planning, and practical application with follow-up actions.

Full-Day PL

Comprehensive full-day professional learning experiences that combine research presentations, collaborative workshops, classroom modelling, and planning time to create sustained shifts in practice.

"Available remotely or face-to-face - supporting schools wherever they are on their improvement journey."

Areas of expertise

Deep knowledge across six interconnected pillars of educational improvement

Science of Learning

Applying cognitive science to strengthen how teachers teach and students learn.

  • Explicit instruction and instructional design
  • Cognitive load theory and its classroom applications
  • Retrieval practice and spaced learning
  • Classroom environments, routines, and cultures
  • Developing a strong Tier 1 instructional model
  • Instructional coaching in all key practices

Science of Reading

Supporting structured literacy implementation aligned with the reading research.

  • Structured literacy - high-impact literacy blocks
  • Big 5 of reading instruction and research
  • Decodable text selection and progression
  • Vocabulary and background knowledge development
  • Assessment-informed reading instruction

Knowledge-Rich Curriculum

Building coherent, connected curricula that develop deep understanding over time.

  • Curriculum coherence and sequencing
  • Schema building and connected content
  • High-quality instructional materials selection
  • Cross-curricular knowledge connections
  • Aligning curriculum, pedagogy, and assessment

MTSS

Designing multi-tiered systems that ensure every student receives the support they need.

  • MTSS frameworks - Tier 1, 2, 3
  • Universal screening and progress monitoring
  • Data-informed decision making at each tier
  • Evidence-based tools (including DIBELS)
  • Building school-wide systems for support

Change Management & Implementation Science

Guiding schools through the complexities of instructional change with structured, evidence-informed approaches.

  • Navigating whole-school instructional reform
  • Building shared understanding and buy-in
  • Developing sustainable implementation plans
  • Supporting leaders through the change process
  • Aligning systems and structures to support new practices

Explicit Instruction

Building adaptive, responsive teaching practices aligned with the science of learning and multi-tiered support.

  • Explicit instruction models and lesson design
  • Adaptive and responsive teaching strategies
  • Integrating explicit instruction within MTSS frameworks
  • Gradual release and scaffolded practice
  • Checking for understanding and responsive adjustments
